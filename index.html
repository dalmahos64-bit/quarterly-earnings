Financial Formula

Return on Equity (ROE):

\[ \mathrm{ROE} = \frac{\mathrm{Net\ Income}}{\mathrm{Shareholders\'\ Equity}} \times 100\% \]

Example: if Net Income = $200M and Equity = $2B, then ROE = \( \frac{200}{2000} \times 100\% = 10\% \)
